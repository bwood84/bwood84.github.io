Love for the ocean and love of American traditional style art is where my inspiration comes for my art. Growing up in and around the water and also in shops watching my mom cut hair had a huge impact on who I am and what I do. Carrying on the tradition of barbering is a craft that I love and enjoy everyday. Surfing never fails to make me smile and my dream of sailing is a never ending learning process. Art is my outlet to share my dreams and what I love with others, series change color and moods just like the seasons...
